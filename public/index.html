Instructions

You will be asked to watch driving videos and annotate what actions that the driver took and why the action was taken.

GOOGLE CHROME IS REQUIRED. The experiment may not work with other browsers.

The entire experiment should take about 60 minutes in total.

During the entire experiment you need to:

  • Pay attention and keep pace with the video. The position of your mouse during the experiment will be automatically recorded every second. You don't need to click anything for giving responses.
  • If you leave the web page, the experiment will end. You can resume the experiment simply by selecting the 'resume progress' option on the participant information page, and put in the first and last name that you entered before. You will need to select the 'First time participating' option if you have not finished any trial. Please do finish the whole experiment within 24 hours after you signed up.
  • If the web page or the video does not response in the middle of the experiment, click on it for several times so that your browser can focus on the web page again.
  • You can receive full credits only if you complete the whole experiment.

If the experiment took you more than 60 minutes or you encountered any problem, please do email the researcher at mandy_chen@berkeley.edu. We appreciate your feedback!
